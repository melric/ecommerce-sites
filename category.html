Products for Dry Skin

Dry skin often feels tight, itchy and uncomfortable and can flake or even crack. This means it’s lacking in moisture and natural oils and may have a weakened surface barrier. Cold temperatures, long hot showers and not using the right skincare products can exacerbate dryness and make fine lines and wrinkles more noticeable.
