KEY INGREDIENTS

  • Exfoliates, smooths and conditions severe dry skin Ideal for knees, elbows, and heels with thick, calloused skin Reduces scaling and roughness associated with psoriasis and severe dry skin Highly emollient formula smooths skin on contact Fragrance-free

  • Details

Apply to wet skin, gently massaging to a lather, covering the entire face. Rinse with water. Use twice daily.

This rich salve contains high strength Alpha Hydroxy Acids (AHA) and Polyhydroxy Acids (PHA) to exfoliate severe dry skin. Contains beeswax, skin conditioning oils and Vitamin E to reduce skin roughness on contact.
